## Testing Protocol

- **1RM Tests:** Proper warm-up, work up to true 1RM with spotter
- **Rep Tests:** No rest between reps, strict form
- **Frequency:** Test every 6-8 weeks
- **Recovery:** Allow 48-72 hours rest before/after testing
